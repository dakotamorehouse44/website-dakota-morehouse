Preparing meals ahead of time!

Why is it so important?

It is important to know what to eat, how much to eat, and when to eat. Here are some reasons why everyone should Meal Prep.

  1. Improves your time managment - you will not have to go out of your way to find food to eat.
  2. Help you stay on track- Prepared and divided into their specific portions you will be more likely to stay on track.
  3. Regulate portion control- Allows you to control the portions of food so you will know the exact calories, carbs, fat, and protein.

Here is an example of an acceptable meal prep.

*Cooking ahead requires containers to store the food in and maybe ziplock bags or some other type of storage device plus a microwave oven or stove top with a non stick pan.

  • Tofu
  • Spinach
  • Brown rice
